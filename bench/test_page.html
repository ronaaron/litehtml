Frequently Asked Questions

What is litehtml?

+

Litehtml is a lightweight HTML/CSS rendering engine written in C++.

How fast is it?

−

Very fast. It can parse and render complex pages in milliseconds.

What platforms are supported?

+

Windows, Linux, macOS, and various embedded platforms.

Is it free?

+

Yes, litehtml is open source under the BSD license.

Can I use it in commercial projects?

+

Absolutely, the BSD license allows commercial use.
